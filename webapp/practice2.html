Dr. Elias Carter spent decades searching the cosmos for signs of intelligence, but when his radio array picked up a rhythmic signal from a distant star, his excitement turned to dread. The transmission, a sequence of prime numbers, ended with a chilling message: “We see you.” Before he could respond, his screen flickered, and new words appeared—not from space, but from his own system: “Do not answer.” His pulse pounded as he realized he wasn’t the first to hear the signal—just the latest.
